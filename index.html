My Favorite Recipes

Avocado toast is one of my favorite recipes because it is quick and simple, but still tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite kind of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Garlic salt
  • Your favorite hot sauce. I like Frank's RedHot or Sriracha.

Instructions

  1. Toast bread
  2. While bread is toasting: Cut avocado in half and discard pit
  3. Scoop out flesh of avocado with a spoon and gently mash onto toast with a fork
  4. Sprinkle on your desired amount of garlic salt (a little goes a long way!)
  5. Add your desired amount of hot sauce
